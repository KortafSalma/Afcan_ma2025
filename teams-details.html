AFCON History

Morocco won their only AFCON title in 1976. They finished as runners-up in 2004 and have reached the semi-finals 4 times (1986, 1988, 2017, 2019).

In AFCON 2025, Morocco topped Group A with 7 points (2 wins, 1 draw) and advanced to the Round of 16 as one of the tournament favorites.
